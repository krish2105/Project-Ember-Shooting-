## Known limitations

- The project has only one approved rifle mesh and one approved pistol mesh. The six entries use those two assets as explicitly tracked stand-ins; this is not six finished weapon models.
- The stairwell is functional generated blockout geometry, not a final rendered modular environment.
- A teleport preview into the stairwell center produced very tight camera collision. Normal entrance-to-exit traversal, AI navigation and packaged encounter completion are not yet certified.
- Final materials, props, doors, signage, weapon-specific rigs/montages, Niagara, layered audio, captions and enemy reactions remain incomplete.
- The build is ad-hoc signed and unnotarized.
